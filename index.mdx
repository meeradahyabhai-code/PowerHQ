## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Contact your PowerHQ representative to receive an API key and have your server's IP address added to the allow-list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your environment">
    Use the **certification** endpoint (`eapi.cert.powerhq.co/graphql`) for integration testing and the **production** endpoint (`eapi.prod.powerhq.co/graphql`) for live traffic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a GraphQL query">
    POST your query with the `x-api-key` header. Request exactly the fields you need and the API returns only those fields as JSON.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display plans and surface enrollment">
    Render plans with their disclosure documents and use the partner-stamped `enrollmentUrl` or `headlessEnrollmentUrl` to hand customers off to the enrollment flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The PowerHQ Plan Data API is available to approved partners only. To request access, contact your PowerHQ representative.
</Note>
